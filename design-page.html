Technology Capabilities:

Digital Transformation

Our comprehensive digital transformation services include advisory for board members and CEOs, optimizing digital customer experiences, managing digital vendors, and advising on digital risk from a CIO's perspective. We guide organizations through the ever-evolving digital landscape.

Blockchain

From cutting-edge research and development to strategic advice and strategy formulation, we help organizations navigate the complex world of blockchain. Additionally, we build blockchain products and platforms, ensuring they stay ahead in this transformative technology.

Cybersecurity

Our expertise in cybersecurity spans governance, risk, and compliance, safeguarding cyber networks, providing managed security services, and seamlessly integrating security into IT infrastructure. We ensure that organizations are protected against evolving cyber threats.

RPA (Robotic Process Automation)

We assess organizations for automation readiness and establish robust governance frameworks to drive the successful implementation of Robotic Process Automation. Efficiency and productivity improvements are within reach.

oT (Internet of Things)

Our IoT services cover everything from expert consulting to secure IoT deployments through established protocols. We also specialize in developing smart connected products and optimizing industrial processes through the Industrial Internet of Things.

Cloud Computing

We help organizations embark on a cloud transformation journey, develop cloud-based solutions, and provide ongoing management and optimization of cloud infrastructure. Seamless migration and efficiency are at the core of our cloud services.

Genome as a Service

Leveraging Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) technology, we expedite genetic, biological, and medical research by handling vast volumes of genetic data. Our services significantly impact research and insights in these fields.

Artificial Intelligence as a Service

Our AI offerings encompass software as a service with packages like R, SPSS, and Matlab, data platform as a service with Hadoop and NoSQL databases, and comprehensive AI training. We empower organizations to harness the power of AI.

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

We provide end-to-end GDPR services, including auditing, certification, training, and expert consulting. Our solutions ensure that organizations achieve and maintain GDPR compliance with confidence.

Telecom

With our strategic guidance, organizations can confidently navigate the 5G landscape, implement Software-Defined Networking (SDN) solutions, adopt Network Function Virtualization (NFV) technology, and enhance their telecom infrastructure through fiber optics. Stay ahead in the telecom industry with our expert guidance.
